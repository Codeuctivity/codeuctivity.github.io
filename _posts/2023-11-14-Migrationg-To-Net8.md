# .NET 8 Released

.NET 8 has been released. Hooray!

This version is the next LTS release, and I'm particularly impressed with the performance boost. The test suite run for PdfjsSharp dropped from ~40s to ~9s. The test suite of Codeuctivity.ImageSharpCompare dropped from 7.3521s -> 3.1666s. Quite impressive!

I plan to begin migrating my unfunded projects, starting with [Codeuctivity.OpenXmlPowerTools](https://www.nuget.org/packages/Codeuctivity.OpenXmlPowerTools). Feel free to contact me at codeuctivity@gmail.com if you are interested in providing funding support.
